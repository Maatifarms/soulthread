# SoulThread Revenue & Monetization Plan

SoulThread is positioned as a **premium, anonymous mental health sanctuary**. Its revenue model should respect the "anonymity first" principle while providing high-value psychological growth tools.

## 1. Premium Learning Series (The "Insight" Tier)
Expand on the success of the *Never Finished* and *Hyperfocus Architect* series.
*   **Model**: Pay-per-series or All-Access Pass.
*   **Pricing**: ₹499 ($5.99) per 30-day bootcamp or ₹1,499 ($17.99) for lifetime access to all current and future series.
*   **Upsell**: PDF Workbooks and Daily SMS/WhatsApp "Savage Reminders" for the Never Finished series.

## 2. Anonymous Counselor Marketplace (The "Sanctuary" Tier)
Connect users with verified psychologists and counselors with 100% anonymity.
*   **Model**: Commission-based (20-30% platform fee).
*   **Feature**: "SoulChat" – anonymous, end-to-end encrypted audio/text sessions.
*   **Revenue**: High-intent users looking for professional help after completing a free series.

## 3. The "Circles" Subscription (The "Community" Tier)
Private, expert-moderated peer support circles.
*   **Model**: Monthly subscription (₹199/month).
*   **Value**: Access to "Elite Focus Circles" or "Resilience Bootcamps" moderated by mental health professionals.
*   **Differentiator**: Unlike free Reddit/Discord, these are safe, troll-free, and professionally guided.

## 4. Corporate Wellness (SoulThread for Business)
Anonymous mental health support for employees.
*   **Target**: High-stress industries (Tech, Finance, Law).
*   **Model**: B2B SaaS (Seat-based pricing).
*   **Value Proposal**: High employee engagement due to the 100% anonymity guarantee (which internal HR tools lack).

## 5. Wellness Marketplace (Affiliate & Direct)
Curated tools that complement the "Attention Sanctuary" vibe.
*   **Products**: Physical journals, blue-light blockers, focus apps, and mental health literature.
*   **Revenue**: 10-15% affiliate commission or direct drop-shipping for SoulThread branded journals.
