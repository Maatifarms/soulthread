# SoulThread Off-Page SEO & Growth Kit

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Off-page SEO is about building **Authority** and **Trust**. Search engines look for "signals" from the rest of the web (backlinks, social mentions, and expert citations).

## 1. High-Authority Backlink Targets
Focus on these platforms to build a diverse link profile:

### Professional & Mental Health Directories
*   **Psychology Today**: (If you have verified counselors, list the service there).
*   **GoodTherapy**: Submit as a mental health resource.
*   **7 Cups / Crisis Text Line**: Reach out for partnership or listing in their "Other Resources" section.

### Community Hubs (The "SoulThread" Vibe)
*   **Reddit (r/MentalHealth, r/Anxiety, r/SelfImprovement)**:

*   **Quora**: Answer questions like "How to stay focused?" and link to the Hyperfocus series.
*   **Medium**: Cross-post the first 3 parts of each series as an article; link the rest to SoulThread.
